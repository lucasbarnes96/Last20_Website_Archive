About Last20

Last20 is a Canadian clean-tech startup that developed innovative plastic-infused pavement technology. By combining traditional pavement materials with low-density polyethylene, they created a sustainable solution that upcycles plastic waste into useful infrastructure.

Founded by siblings with a vision for environmental sustainability, Last20 pioneered the way to a more sustainable future through innovative pavement solutions.
