About Last20

Sustainable Innovation

We create plastic-infused pavement using low-density polyethylene (LDPE). We are dedicated to reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainability in the construction industry.

Last20 was founded by Lucas and Lauren, united by their passion for sustainability and innovation. Their journey is fueled by a commitment to revolutionize the pavement industry by upcycling plastic.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the pavement industry by creating sustainable, plastic-infused asphalt solutions that reduce waste and promote environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

A world where plastic waste is transformed into valuable infrastructure, paving the way to a more sustainable future for generations to come.

Our Technology

Using low-density polyethylene (LDPE), we create durable pavement solutions that perform as well as traditional asphalt while helping to reduce plastic waste.

Learn About Plastic Pavement

What is Plastic Pavement?

Plastic pavement is an innovative construction material that incorporates recycled plastic waste into traditional asphalt mixtures, creating a more sustainable and durable road surface.

Environmental Benefits

By upcycling plastic waste, we reduce landfill burden, decrease petroleum consumption in asphalt production, and create long-lasting infrastructure solutions.

Performance Advantages

Plastic-infused pavement offers improved durability, better resistance to cracking, enhanced flexibility in temperature changes, and reduced maintenance needs.

Research & Development

Our ongoing research focuses on optimizing plastic content ratios, testing long-term performance, and developing new applications for various infrastructure projects.

Implementation Process

We work with municipalities and contractors to integrate our plastic pavement solutions into existing construction workflows with minimal disruption.

Quality Assurance

All our plastic pavement solutions undergo rigorous testing to ensure they meet or exceed industry standards for safety, durability, and performance.
