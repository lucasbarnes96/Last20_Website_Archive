About Last20

[Founders Photo]

Sustainable Innovation

We create plastic-infused pavement using low-density polyethylene (LDPE). We are dedicated to reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainability in the construction industry.

Last20 was founded by Lucas and Lauren, united by their passion for sustainability and innovation. Their journey is fueled by a commitment to revolutionize the pavement industry by upcycling plastic.
